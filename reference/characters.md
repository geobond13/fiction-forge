## Instructions

1. Start with your POV character(s) and antagonist(s).
2. At minimum, capture **voice notes** and **arc summary** — these matter most
   for AI-assisted writing consistency.
3. Update the **Status** field as the story progresses (Active, Injured, Dead, etc.).
4. Link detailed profiles: `See characters/name.md for full profile.`
