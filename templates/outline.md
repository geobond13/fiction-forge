## Instructions

1. **Number chapters sequentially** using two-digit zero-padded numbers (00, 01, 02...).
   Chapter filenames should match: `book/01_Chapter_Title.md`.
2. **POV column** should name the viewpoint character. Use "Narrator" for
   omniscient or frame-story sections.
3. **Update Status** as you work. This lets AI tools know which chapters are
   stable enough for continuity checking.
4. **Notes column** is for brief flags: foreshadowing plants, key revelations,
   pacing concerns, dependencies on other chapters.
5. If you **cut a chapter**, change its status to `cut` rather than deleting the row.
   This preserves the history of structural decisions.
